Introduction to Known
#####################

`Known <https://withknown.com>`_ is an open publishing and collaboration platform that allows anyone to share their stories using many types
of media. Whether you are publishing as a group, or just as an individual, Known allows you to post to a site that you
really own, while reaching your audience whever they might be.

This is the canonical documentation for the project.
